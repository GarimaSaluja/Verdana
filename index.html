🧭 How to Use Verdana

Everything you need to know to get started

01
📸

Identify Your Plant

Head to the Identify Plant tab, upload a photo of any plant. Answer 4 quick questions about your environment and experience — and receive a personalized care guide powered by AI.

02
💚

Build Your Collection

Save identified plants or browse the Encyclopedia to add plants to My Plants. Each plant has its own detailed care profile with watering, light, soil and fertilizer info.

03
📅

Use the Care Calendar

Add watering, fertilizing, pruning tasks to your Calendar. The calendar automatically highlights task days so you never forget your plant's needs.

04
☀️

Scan Room Light

Use the Light Meter to simulate your room's light level. Select your window type and get instant plant recommendations for that exact spot in your home.

05
🤖

Chat with Verdana AI

The chatbot (bottom right) is your personal plant expert! Ask it anything — "Why are my leaves turning yellow?", "How often to water a cactus?", or "Best plant for a dark room?"

06
📜

Track Your History

Every action you take — identifying plants, saving them, completing tasks — is logged in your History tab. You can review or clear it anytime.

💡 Pro Tips

🌿 Most indoor plants prefer bright indirect light, not direct sun
💧 Overwatering is the #1 cause of plant death — check soil first
🌡️ Keep plants away from heating/cooling vents
🌱 Spring & summer = growing season, reduce care in winter
✂️ Remove dead leaves to redirect energy to healthy growth
🪴 Only repot when roots are visibly escaping the drainage holes
